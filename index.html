Biography

I am currently a PhD student at the Interdisciplinary Graduate Program in Quantitative Biosciences of Georgia Institute of Technology starting from Fall 2023. I work at the intersection of machine learning and neuromechanics.

I obtained my Master’s Degree in School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. I obtained my BEng in Software Engineering and BSc in Biological Sciences at Sichuan University, China, in 2021.

I was a Research Assistant in School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University working with Hesheng Wang, PhD. I was an Assistant Investigator in Lab of Neural Interface, Institute of Neuroscience, Chinese Academy of Sciences, working with Zhengtuo Zhao, PhD. I was an Undergraduate Research Assistant in the Department of Neurology, Massachusetts General Hospital, Harvard Medical School, working with Wei-Long Zheng, PhD. I was also a Summer Fellow in School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering at University of Essex, United Kingdom, working with Junhua Li, PhD.
