Biography

I am currently a PhD student at the Interdisciplinary Graduate Program in Quantitative Biosciences of Georgia Institute of Technology starting from 2023 Fall. I work at the intersection of machine learning and neuroscience.

Before PhD, I was a Master’s Student in School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology.

Before Georgia Tech, I was a Research Assistant in School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University working with Hesheng Wang PhD,. I obtained my BEng in Software Engineering and BSc in Biological Sciences at Sichuan University, China, in 2021.

From 01/2021-10/2021, I was a Assistant Investigator in Lab of Neural Interface, Institute of Neuroscience, Chinese Academy of Sciences, China, working with Zhengtuo Zhao, PhD. From 04-2020-01/2021, I was an Undergraduate Research Assistant in the Department of Neurology, Massachusetts General Hospital, Harvard Medical School, working with Wei-Long Zheng, PhD. From 07/2019-09-2019, I was a Deep Learning Summer Intern at School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering at University of Essex, United Kingdom, working with Junhua Li, PhD.
